    Amazing!!! I had their takeout now 3 days in a row and I still cannot get enough of it. I am very food intolerant and restaurant foods often don't sit well with me. With Burma Eat I did not have the problem. I had the Pad Thai with Shrimp, Spicy Noodles (they are spicy!) and the Veggie Curry (lots and lots of different veggies with a little kick from the jalapeos) and tried the potato beef dish. Samusas are great and the Patha Veggie appetizer as well. The food was actually hot when we got it and the portion size very generous. I am addicted to their iced milk tea. I would call the food a mix of Indian and Thai food. Very distinct flavors created with fresh ingredients. Go and support this new business. I hope they will stay around for years to come.
