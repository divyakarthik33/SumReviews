    This is a salad place. They offer salads. If you didn't know, salads are healthy-ish choices for lunch that get exponentially better when you add meat, cheese, fruit, and nuts to them.

    Here at Mixt, you should probably go with one of the seasonal options (usually very solid), although the Orchard is a winner too because it offers two types of meat (chicken and bacon), cheese (cheddar), fruit (apple), nuts (almonds), AND avocado. If your company is buying, get a cookie too.

    One of my main issues with Mixt is that when you take a salad out, it comes crammed into a container with absolutely no room left, meaning that it's impossible to toss your salad up without getting all the delicious toppings everywhere.

    I suppose it is somewhat ironic that one of my main issues with a place called Mixt is that their salads aren't mixed. Things that make you go, "Hmmm."*

    Overall though, Mixt is a solid if fairly boring choice for lunch. I've probably eaten here more times than I probably should have, considering I don't even live in this city.
