SumReviews

Summary views - of some reviews.

Want to get perspective but tired of scrolling through the comments section?

Now you can get the most out of any lengthy, biased, and unorganized discussion section.

SumReviews is a comment summarizer tool designed to be compatible with any discussion forum. SumReviews highlights the most prominent topics the people are talking about and summarizes them for you. Compare what multiple platforms have to say about a product at once; since this application will be taking the notes for you. Gauge the public sentiment and opinion to make your decision faster, simpler, and more accurate.
